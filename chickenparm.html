Chicken Parmesan

Chicken Parmigiana, more commonly known as Chicken Parmesan, is an Italian-American dish made with breaded chicken cutlets, tomato sauce, and melted cheese.
click for full size
  • Prep Time: 25 minutes
  • Cook Time: 45 minutes
  • Yield: 3-4 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 Tbsp salt and ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup neutral oil (for frying)
  • 1 cup Italian-American Marinara sauce(see notes1)
  • 2 ounces Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 8 ounces fresh Mozzarella cheese, cut into small chunks
  • freshly minced parsley and basil (see notes2)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Split chicken breasts in half horizontally. Pat dry and cover with salt.
  3. Place flour, black pepper, and garlic powder into a shallow dish. Stir to combine.
  4. Into a second shallow dish, crack and whisk the 2 eggs.
  5. Into a third shallow dish, place the breadcrumbs.
  6. Dredge each chicken breast in the flour mixture, shaking off any excess.
  7. Dip each floured chicken breast into the egg mixture, then coat in the breadcrumbs.
  8. Heat neutral oil over medium-high heat. When hot, add breaded chicken breasts.
  9. Cook chicken breasts till golden-brown on both sides, flipping after 3-4 minutes.
  10. Remove chicken from oil and place on baking sheet, sprinkling with half of the grated Parmesan cheese.
  11. Pour Marinara sauce over each chicken breast, lightly coating the top, then place Mozzarella on top.
  12. Transfer to oven and cook until cheese is melted and slightly browned, about 15 minutes.
  13. Remove from oven and top with remaining Parmesan cheese and minced herbs, serve immediately.

Notes

  1. You can use a jar of your favorite tomato sauce for this recipe.
  2. Other herbs you can use include Sage, Chervil, Chives, and Oregano.
